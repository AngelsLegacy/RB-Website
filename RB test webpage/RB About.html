About Us

Rattlebag - a unique, intimate cocktail experience.
No Reservations.
Walk ins only.

Rattlebag was named the Best Bar in Northern Ireland at the 2025 CLASS Bar Awards - one of the industry's most reputable awards.
We also hold a prestigious 1 PIN of excellence from The Pinnacle Guide and were the first in Northern Ireland to be awarded one.
It was an honour to be one of only 37 bars in the world to receive this award during its initial launch and to be recognised on a global scale is a real credit to our team!
